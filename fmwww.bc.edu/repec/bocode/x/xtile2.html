help for ^xtile2^

^xtile2^ creates a new variable that categorizes exp by its subgroup
        quantiles.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------


  ^xtile2^ newvar ^=^ exp [weight] [^if^ exp] [^in^ range]
    ^,^ by(varlist) [ ^n^quantiles^(^#^)^  ^alt^def ]




Options
--------


  ^nquantiles(^#^)^ specifies the number of quantiles.    
  ^by(varlist)^ specifies subgroups within which the category will be
generated.


Examples
---------
  . ^xtile2 quart = bp if age>=25, nq(4) by(sex race)^ 
  


Also see
---------
