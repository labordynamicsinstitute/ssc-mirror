Equation of a straight line


    esli , p1(#y1 #x1) p2(#y2 #x2)


Description


    esli provides the equation of the straight line passing through
          two specified points.


Options


    p1(#y1 #x1) specifies the coordinates of the first point.


    p2(#y2 #x2) specifies the coordinates of the second point.


Examples


     . esli, p1(3 760) p2(6 5724) 
     . esli, p1(3 573) p2(6 4944) 
     . tw function `r(equation)'
     . tw function `r(equation)', range(0 6000)
