Output The Equation of a Regression




Description




    equation gives the equation after a regression.




Examples




    . sysuse auto


    . reg price weight length i.foreign


    . equation


    . reg price weight length i.foreign i.rep78 mpg trunk turn displacement
        gear_ratio


    . equation


    . webuse nlswork


    . xtreg ln_w grade age c.age#c.age ttl_exp c.ttl_exp#c.ttl_exp tenure
        c.tenure#c.tenure 2.race not_smsa south, be


    . equation
