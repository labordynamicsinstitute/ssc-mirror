Calculate Earned Income Tax Credit receipts




        eitc inc invinc child year , generate(newvar)






Description


    eitc calculates the amount of Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) receipts
    received by a married couple filing jointly with a specified total annual
    income inc, investment income invinc and number of dependent children
    present child in any year year between 1975 and 2005.


    inc and invinc should be recorded in current dollars.


    To be counted as a dependent, the EITC requires that a child be under 19
    at the end of year or under 24 and a full-time student.


    year refers to the actual calendar year – users should remember that
    questions in datasets such as the Census and CPS refer to income in the
    previous calendar year.




Options


    generate(newvar) is required and specifies the name of the new variable
        to be created.
