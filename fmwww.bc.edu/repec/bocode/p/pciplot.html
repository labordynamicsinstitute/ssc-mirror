Title


    pciplot -- Plot pointwise confidence intervals




Syntax


        pciplot yvar {yvar_lci yvar_uci} | se_yvar} xvar [if] [in] [, options
            ]




    options are any options appropriate to graph, twoway.






Description


    pciplot produces plots of pointwise confidence intervals for yvar against
    a continuous variable, xvar. Either the s.e., se_yvar, of yvar or the
    confidence limits, yvar_lci and yvar_uci, may be supplied. The CIs are
    rendered as a shaded area and the relationship between yvar and xvar is
    plotted as a line.




Examples


    . pciplot y y_se x
    . gen y_lci = y - 1.96 * y_se
    . gen y_uci = y + 1.96 * y_se
    . pciplot y y_lc y_uci x, yline(1) legend(off) saving(graph, replace)
