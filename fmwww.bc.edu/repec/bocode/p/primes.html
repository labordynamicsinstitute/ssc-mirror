help primes


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Title


    primes --     Generating prime numbres




Syntax


        primes #, clear




Description


    primes creates a dataset containing the prime numbers up to the specified
    number. Note that clear is a required option, as primes destroys the
    dataset currently in memory.


Example


    . primes 20, clear
    . list


     +-------+
     | prime |
     |-------|
  1. |     2 |
  2. |     3 |
  3. |     5 |
  4. |     7 |
  5. |    11 |
     |-------|
  6. |    13 |
  7. |    17 |
  8. |    19 |
     +-------+
