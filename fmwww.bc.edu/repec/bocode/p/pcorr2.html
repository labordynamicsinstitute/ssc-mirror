help for pcorr2
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Partial and semipartial correlation coefficients


        pcorr2 varname1 varlist [weight] [if exp] [in range]




    by ... : may be used with pcorr2; see help by.


    aweights and fweights are allowed; see help weights.




Description


    pcorr2 displays the partial and semipartial correlation coefficients of
    varname1 with each variable in varlist, holding the other variables in
    varlist constant. It also displays the squared partial and semipartial
    correlations.  It is a modified and enhanced version of Stata's pcorr
    command.




Example


    . pcorr2 price mpg weight foreign
