help clickout


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Title


    clickout -- produces a list of clickable files for your clicking pleasure




Syntax


    clickout [file extension] [using <directory>]




Description


    clickout provides a fast and easy way to open the files contents of
    current directory (cd) or any folder for your clicking pleasure.




Examples


    * assuming you have a file with .xml extension in your current
    directory:
    clickout xml


    * assuming you have anything in your current directory:
    clickout
    clickout *


    * assuming you have anything in your "c:/" folder:
    clickout * using "c:/"




Remarks


    No guaratee that all files will successfully open.
