Converts datasets from Web of Science data from wide to long
----------------------------------------------------------


 ^wosadress^ [filename]
 
 
Description
-----------


^wosaddress^
Takes datasets created using ^wosload^ and converts them from long
to wide format.  


      




Note
----


For a full description of this program see:


Bornmann, L. & Ozimek, A. (in press). Stata commands for 
importing bibliometric data and processing author address 
information. Journal of Informetrics 


This program requires ^egenmore^ and ^renvars^.






Examples
--------


. ^cd "C:\Documents"^
. ^wosload web_of_science_file1 web_of_science_file2 web_of_science_file3^
. ^wosaddress^




. ^cd "C:\Documents"^
. ^wosload web_of_science_file1 web_of_science_file2 web_of_science_file3^
. ^save wos_data.dta, replace^
. ^clear^ 
. ^wosaddress wos_data.dta^
