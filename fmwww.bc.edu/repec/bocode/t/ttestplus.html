-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Help for ttestplus
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Description


ttestplus automates and tabulates t-tests on one or more dimensions across two
populations indicated by a categorical variable. It can also cut a continuous
variable at a specified value to serve as a categorical.


Alternatively, ttestplus automates and tabulates t-tests on one analysis
dimension across a population with characteristics defined by two or more
binary categories.


Syntax


ttestplus varlist [if] [in], 
        by(groupvar | varlist) 
        [cut(value | mean | median)]
        [cluster(groupvar)]
        [t] [se]


    Options               Description
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    * by(groupvar)        Variable defining groups for comparison by varlist.
    * by(varlist)         List of binary variables for comparison. Cannot be
                            combined with multiple analysis dimensions or
                            with cut.
    cut()                 Cuts a continuous groupvar at the specified value,
                            at its mean, or at its median.
    cluster               Cluster results using the specified categorical
                            variable. Requires the most recent version of 
                            clttest.
    t                     Returns t-statistics instead of p-statistics.
    se                    Returns standard errors below group means.
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    * Exactly one version of by() is required.


Examples


. sysuse nlsw88
(NLSW, 1988 extract)


. ttestplus married grade wage, by(collgrad)


             |   Group 1    Group 2     p-Stat 
-------------+---------------------------------
     married |  .6406068   .6466165   .4003468 
       grade |  11.97371   16.71992          0 
        wage |  6.910561   10.52606   2.63e-38 


. ttestplus married grade wage, by(hours) cut(median) se


hours cut at 40 (median)

          SE |  .0869087   .0669262          . 
        wage |  6.492085   8.430878   1.58e-14 
          SE |  .1552967   .1631842          .
