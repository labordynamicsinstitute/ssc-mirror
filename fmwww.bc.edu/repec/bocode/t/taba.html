Tabulation of frequencies
-------------------------


    ^taba^ [varlist] [weight] [^if^ exp] [^in^ range]


Description
-----------


^taba^ shows the frequencies of values in varlist. Frequencies of
present values are shown as percents of the total frequency of present
values. Frequencies of missing values are shown as percents of the
total frequency of all values.


Examples
--------


    . ^taba^
