Cross-tabulation of labelled and unlabelled variable
- ----------------------------------------------------


        ^tablab^ varname [^if^ exp] [^in^ range] [^,^ tabulate_options]


Description
- -----------


^tablab^ takes a variable and cross-tabulates it with a copy of itself.
This may be useful if and only if the variable is a labelled numeric
variable and you wish to examine its labelled and unlabelled versions
simultaneously.


Options
- -------


tabulate_options are those legitimate with ^tabulate^ var1 var2.


Example
- -------


    . ^tablab foreign^
