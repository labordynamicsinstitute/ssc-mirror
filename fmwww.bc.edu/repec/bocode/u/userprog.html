Install user-written programs from http://www.stata.com/users 
-------------------------------------------------------------


    ^userprog^ [who [what ^install^ ]] 


    
Description
-----------


^userprog^ shows you what is available for download at or via 


@http://www.stata.com/users@


and allows you to install any package you desire. 


^userprog^ 


is a shortcut for 


^net from http://www.stata.com/users/^ 


^userprog^ who 


is a shortcut for 


^net from http://www.stata.com/users/^who 


or alternatively for 


^net from http://www.stata.com/users/^ 
^net link^ who 


^userprog^ who what


is a shortcut for 


^qui net from http://www.stata.com/users/^who 
^net describe^ what 


or alternatively for 


^qui net from http://www.stata.com/users/^ 
^qui net link^ who 
^net describe^ what     


^userprog^ who what ^install^ 


is a shortcut for 


^qui net from http://www.stata.com/users/^who 
^net describe^ what 
^net install^ what 


or alternatively for 


^qui net from http://www.stata.com/users/^ 
^qui net link^ who 
^net describe^ what
^net install^ what 




Remarks
-------


^userprog^ is not smart. You must type who and what exactly as used 
at the website in question. It must be ^wgould^, not ^wwg^ or ^Bill Gould^ 
or any other version. It must be ^ssc-ideas^, when relevant. 


     
Examples
--------


    . ^userprog^ 
    . ^userprog wgould^
    . ^userprog wgould gennorm^
    . ^userprog jweesie iia^ 
    . ^userprog jweesie iia install^ 
    . ^userprog ssc-ideas hotdeck^ 
    . ^userprog ssc-ideas hotdeck install^
