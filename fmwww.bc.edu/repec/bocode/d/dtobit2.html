Estimate a tobit model and provide marginal effects at observed censoring rate
------------------------------------------------------------------------------


       ^dtobit2^ -- standard tobit syntax --


Description
-----------


Estimates a tobit model and provides a table of marginal effects evaluated at
the observed censoring rate of the dependent variable.  The marginal effects
are computed for the dependent variable conditional on the censoring and on
the unconditional expected value of the dependent variable.
