help for diffpi
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Difference expressed as a percentage


    diffpi #1 #2 [, format(%fmt) ]


Description


    diffpi provides the percentage of #1 to be added or subtracted,
          depending on its positive or negative sign, to #1 to get
          #2.  It is an immediate command, see help immed.


Options


    format(%fmt) specifies the display format for presenting numbers.
        format(%3.2f) is the default; see help format.


Examples


     . diffpi 100 20
     . diffpi 80 100
     . diffpi 46 43
     . diffpi 46 43 , f(%5.4f)
     . diffpi 1.1 1.5
