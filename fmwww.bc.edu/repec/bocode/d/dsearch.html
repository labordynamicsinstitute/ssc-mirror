-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
help for dsearch
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Finds variables with given variable label


        dsearch word


Description 


dsearch finds variables with given variable label and describes them on the
screen.  This may be usefull in datasets with lots of variables. In such
datasets one sometimes remember the variable but not the variable name. In this
situation finding the variable name with desearch is easy.


Examples


        . dsearch headroom


        . dsearch CIRCLE make heaDRoom
