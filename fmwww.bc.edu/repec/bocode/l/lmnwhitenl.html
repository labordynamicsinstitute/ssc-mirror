        +-------+
    ----+ Title +------------------------------------------------------------


lmnwhitenl: NLS Non Normality White Test


        +-------------------+
    ----+ Table of Contents +------------------------------------------------


     Syntax
     Options
     Description
     Saved Results
     References


 *** Examples


     Authors


        +--------+
    ----+ Syntax +-----------------------------------------------------------


   lmnwhitenl depvar [if] [in] [weight] , fun(expression)
 
     [ initial(init_val) variables(varlist) vce(vcetype ]


        +---------+
    ----+ Options +----------------------------------------------------------


  depvar               Dependent Variable


  variables(varlist)   Independent Variables in model


  initial(init_val)    Parameters (initial) Starting Values


  fun(expression)      RHS Mathematical Expression


    SE/Robust
    vce(vcetype)      vcetype may be gnr, robust, cluster clustvar,
                        bootstrap, jackknife, hac kernel, hc2, or hc3


        +-------------+
    ----+ Description +------------------------------------------------------


    lmnwhitenl computes Non Linear Least Squares Non Normality White Test.


        Ho: Normality - Ha: Non Normality
        - White IM Test


        +---------------+
    ----+ Saved Results +----------------------------------------------------


    lmnwhitenl saves the following in e():


   e(lmnw)         White IM Test
   e(lmnwp)        White IM Test P-Value


        +------------+
    ----+ References +-------------------------------------------------------


    Damodar Gujarati (1995) "Basic Econometrics" 3rd Edition, McGraw Hill,
        New York, USA.


    William E. Griffiths, R. Carter Hill and George G. Judge (1993) "Learning
        and Practicing Econometrics", John Wiley & Sons, Inc., New York, USA;
        721-725.


        +----------+
    ----+ Examples +---------------------------------------------------------


clear all


sysuse lmnwhitenl.dta , clear


gen ly=ln(y)


lmnwhitenl ly , fun({B0}+{B1}*k+{B2}*l)


lmnwhitenl ly, fun({B}-({H}/{R})*ln({D}*l^(-{R})+(1-{D})*k^(-{R}))) in(B 1 H 1 
> R 1 D 0.5)
