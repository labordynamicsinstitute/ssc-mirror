        +-------+
    ----+ Title +------------------------------------------------------------


 lmeg: Augmented Engle-Granger Cointegration Test at Higher Order AR(p)


        +--------+
    ----+ Syntax +-----------------------------------------------------------


  lmeg depvar indepvars [if] [in] , [lags(#) aux(varlist) noconstant coll]
 


        +---------+
    ----+ Options +----------------------------------------------------------


  lags(#)          Order of Lag Length; default is lag(1)


  noconstant       suppress constant term


  coll             Keep Collinear Variables


   aux(varlist) add Auxiliary Variables into model without converting them to
                    lagged variables, i.e., dummy variables, time trend,
                    ...etc.


        +-------------+
    ----+ Description +------------------------------------------------------


  lmeg computes Augmented Engle-Granger Cointegration Test at Higher Order
  AR(p)


        +---------------+
    ----+ Saved Results +----------------------------------------------------
    lmeg saves the following in r():


   r(lmegz_0)      Engle-Granger Cointegration z Test
   r(lmegzp_0)     Engle-Granger Cointegration z Test P-Value


   r(lmegt_0)      Engle-Granger Cointegration t Test
   r(lmegtp_0)     Engle-Granger Cointegration t Test P-Value


   r(lmeg_#)       Augmented Engle-Granger Cointegration t Test for order(#)
   r(lmegp_#)      Augmented Engle-Granger Cointegration t Test P-Value for ord
> er(#)


        +----------+
    ----+ Examples +---------------------------------------------------------


        clear all


        db lmeg


        sysuse lmeg.dta , clear


        lmeg y1 y2 , lags(1)


        lmeg y1 y2 , lags(1) aux(t)


        lmeg y1 y2 , lags(1) aux(t y3)


        lmeg y1 y2 , lags(2)


        return list


. clear all
. sysuse lmeg.dta , clear
. lmeg y1 y2 , lags(2)
