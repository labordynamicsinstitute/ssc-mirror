-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Erase temporary datasets and global macros created by miset


        mireset




Description


    mireset erases the temporary datasets, _mitemp1.dta, ..., _mitempm.dta,
    created by miset (if they exist in the current working directory), as
    well as clearing the global macros created by miset.




Authors


    Ning Li, Philip Greenwood, and John Carlin, Clinical Epidemiology &
    Biostatistics Unit, Murdoch Children's Research Institute and University
    of Melbourne. Email:  jbcarlin@unimelb.edu.au
