-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
help for mitestparm                                             (SJ3-3: st0000)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Perform multivariate Wald test on multiply imputed datasets


        mitestparm varlist




Description


    mitestparm is the mi version of the post-estimation command testparm.  It
    obtains an approximate F-test to test the hypothesis that the specified
    coefficients are all equal to zero, analogous to the standard Wald test.
    The testparm options equal and equation have not yet been implemented.




Remarks


    mitestparm is one of the suite of mi commands. Since mitestparm applies
    only to datasets that have been created by miset and estimates that have
    been obtained using mifit, it can only be issued after miset and mifit.




Examples


    . mifit: xi: logistic alcdhi i.wave i.smk i.ciscat
    . mitestparm _Iwave*
