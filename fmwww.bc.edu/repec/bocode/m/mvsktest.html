help mvsktest


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Multivariate skewness and kurtosis


    mvsktest --   Multivariate skewness and kurtosis test




Syntax


        mvsktest varlist [if exp] [in range]






Description


    mvsktest performs an (asymptotic) test for multivariate skewness and
    kurtosis using the results in Mardia (1970). Separate p-values are given
    for the multivariate skewness and multivariate kurtosis.


Example


    . sysuse auto


    . mvsktest wei len turn head trunk


    Multvariate skewness b_1,p = 5.0866
    chi2(35)= 62.7349; Prob > chi2 = 0.0027


    Multivariate kurtosis b_2,p = 34.3138
    z = 0.1270; Prob > |z| = 0.8989


    . mvsktest wei len turn head trunk disp for


    Multvariate skewness b_1,p = 15.9989
    chi2(84)= 197.3198; Prob > chi2 = 0.0000
    #D.f. < #obs.; asymptotic distribution may not be accurate


    Multivariate kurtosis b_2,p = 66.3341
    z = 1.9213; Prob > |z| = 0.0547






See also
Online:  help for sktest

                     with Applications.  Biometrika, 57 (3), pp. 519-530
