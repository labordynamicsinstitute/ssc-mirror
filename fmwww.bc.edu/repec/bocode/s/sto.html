Toggle ^set trace^
----------------


    ^sto^ 


Description
-----------


^sto^ (acronym for both ^set trace on^ and ^set trace off^) toggles tracing 
on and off. 


If ^trace^ is on, ^sto^ turns it off, and vice versa. 




Examples
--------


    . ^sto^
