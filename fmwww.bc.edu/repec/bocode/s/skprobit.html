help skprobit
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Title


     Performs Lagrange Multiplier Test for Normality for Probit model






Syntax


        skprobit depvar indepvars [if] [in]






Description


    skprobit Performs a Lagrange Multiplier Test for the Normality of the
    residuals of a Probit model of depvar on indepvars.






Citation


    skprobit is not an official Stata command. The usual disclimers apply:
    all errors and imperfections in this package are mine and all comments
    are very welcome.






Return values


   Scalars
       r(N)               Number of observations used
       r(chi2_p)          p value of the chi-squared statistic
       r(chi2)            Chi-squared statistic






Examples


    sysuse auto


    probit foreign weight mpg


    skprobit foreign weight mpg


    The result shows that the Ho (Normality) hypothesis of the residuals is
        rejected in this example. The residuals are not normal in this case.


    webuse lbw


    probit low age lwt race smoke ptl ht ui


    skprobit low age lwt race smoke ptl ht ui


    The result shows that the Ho (Normality) hypothesis of the residuals is
        not rejected in this example. Hence the residuals are normal in this
        case.


    webuse union


    probit union age grade not_smsa


    skprobit union age grade not_smsa


    The result shows that the Ho (Normality) hypothesis of the residuals is
        rejected in this example. The residuals are not normal in this case.
