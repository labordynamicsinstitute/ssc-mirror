isko6888


        isko8868 newvarname , isko(varname)


Description


The isko package consists of a series of programs to recode ISCO-88
(International Classification of Occupations) codes into other occupational
scales. Use the isco package to recode ISCO-68 occupational codes.  These
programs are Stata versions of the SPSS recode files by Harry B.G. Ganzeboom
and Donald J. Treiman, which are available at http://www.fss.uu.nl/soc/hg/ismf.


isko8868 transforms ISCO-88 codes into ISCO-68 codes; cf ILO (1968, 1990).  The
argument for the isko commands must be a new variable name in which the recoded
occupational scale is to be placed. The isko option is used specify the
variable containing the ISCO-68 occupational codes.


Options


isko specifies the variable to be recoded.  This must be a 4 digit integer
    containing ISCO-88 occupational codes.


References


Ganzeboom, H.B.G., de Graaf, P.M. & D.J. Treiman. 1992.  A Standard
    International Socio-Economic Index of Occupational Status.  Social Science
    Research 21: 1-56.


ILO (International Labour Office). 1968.  International Standard Classification
    of Occupations, revised edition.  Geneva: International Labour Office.
