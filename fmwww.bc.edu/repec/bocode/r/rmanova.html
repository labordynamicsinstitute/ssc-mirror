syntax ^rmanova^ y_var subject_var within_var [between_var1 between_var2 betwee
> n_var3]


computes within and between sum of squares, with error for each as interaction




example:


^rmanova bp patnum time drug^
                                                  
will compute the ANOVA table for:
effect of independent factor(s) drug1 drug2 drug 3 (between effects) on depende
> nt var bp,
measured on subjects patnum, adjusted for repeated measures over time (within e
> ffect).


Data should be in LONG format:


observation     patnum  time    bp      drug
- -----------   ------  ----    --      ----
1               1       1       100     0
2               1       2       110     0
3               1       3       105     0
4               1       4       110     0
5               2       1       80      1
6               2       2       90      1
7               2       3       95      1
8               2       3       90      1
.
.
.
