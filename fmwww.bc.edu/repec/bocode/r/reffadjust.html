Title


     reffadjust -- Introduction to random effects adjustment commands




Description


    The reffadjust package provides postestimation commands to perform
    adjustment of random effects estimates.


    The reffadjust commands are


        reffadjustsim        simulating from the distribution of random
                               effect variances and covariances
        reffadjust4nlcom     regression coefficient formula to pass to nlcom


    Since multilevel models including random effects, such as those
    implemented in MLwiN (Rasbash et al 2009) and xtmixed, return estimates
    of the variances and covariances of the random effects and the
    corresponding variances and covariances of these estimates we can use
    these to estimate adjusted coefficients.


    The approach is described in more detail in Macdonald-Wallis et al.
    (2011, submitted).  When the multilevel model is a multivariate response
    longitudinal model the approach is related to Granger causality (Granger,
    1969).


    The commands run with estimates from runmlwin or chains from runmlwin by
    mcmcsum (Leckie and Charlton, 2011), xtmixed, xtmelogit, and xtmepoisson.
