basic syntax:
rwg var, scale(#) model(#)


Description:
Rwg calculates single(model 1) and multiple items(model 2) within group interra
> ter reliability. The program requires data in "long" format with panel setup 
> (tsset). The program makes use of panel ids to identify raters and ratees, th
> erefore, it is very important to correctly specify items and judges in panel 
> setup. 


Multiple-items estimator (model 2) and 5 points response scale are set as defau
> lt.


*Model(1): Single-item estimator


 Rwg(i)= 1 - [Sx^2/seu]




*Model(2): Multiple-item estimator


              J[1-(Sx^2/seu)]
 Rwg(j)= ---------------------------
         J[1-(Sx^2/seu)]+ (Sx^2/seu)


Where Rwg(i) and Rwg(j) are within-group interrater reliability for single and 
> multiple items. Sx^2 is the observed variance of ratings, J is number of item
> s (model 2 eq.), seu=(A^2-1)/12 and `A' is response scale(e.g. 5 or 7 points 
> likert scale).


Examples:
----------
tsset items judges


rwg x, scale(7) model(1)
rwg x, scale(9) model(1)


rwg x, scale(7) model(2)
rwg x, scale(9) model(2)
