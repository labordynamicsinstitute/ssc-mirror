Pearson's r Effect Size Computation for Analysis of Variance (ANOVA)
----------------------------------
 
^reffect^ var




Description
-----------


^reffect^ performs Pearson's r effect size computations for main effects and 
        interactions after running ^anova^.
       




Examples
--------


 . ^anova y b^
 . ^reffect b^
 
  ^/* test for effect size of interactions */^
 . ^anova y a b a#b^
 . ^reffect a#b^
