help for rvlrplot7
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Residuals vs lagged residuals for time series data 


        rvlrplot7 [ , graph_options ]




Description


rvlrplot7 plots residuals from the last model versus lagged (i.e. lag 1)
residuals. Data must have been tsset previously.


More precisely, residuals are whatever predict, res produces after a model. The
plot is restricted to the estimation sample.


rvlrplot7 is a renamed clone of rvlrplot 1.0.0 which is for Stata 7. Stata 8
users should use rvlrplot 2.0.0 or later.




Remarks


The correlation between residuals and lagged residuals is calculated quietly.
This may be retrieved by return list and then (say) used in a second pass
giving the correlation on the graph.




Options


graph_options are options of graph, twoway.




Examples


    . tsset time


    . regress whatever time


    . rvlrplot7
