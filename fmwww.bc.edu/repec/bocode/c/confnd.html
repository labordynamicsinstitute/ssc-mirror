-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
help for confnd
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


plot and display change-in-effect estimates after estimation 


        confnd [, eform forward lockterms(varlist) format(%fmt) vertical
                 graph_options ]


Description


    confnd plots and displays effect estimates using stepwise
    change-in-estimate method for assessing confounding. The first
    independent variable is the exposure of interest and all other
    independent variables are potential confounders.


Options


    eform reports the estimated coefficients transformed to odds ratios,
        hazard ratios, relative risk i.e., exp(b) rather than b.  Confidence
        intervals are similarly transformed.


    format(%fmt) specifies the display format for presenting numbers in graph
        and table.  format(%9.0g) is the default.
    forward specifies the forward method, the backward method is the default.
    lockterms(varlist) specifies variables to be included in all models.
    vertical plots vertical spike. The default is horizontal
    graph_options refers to options of graph twoway scatter.


Examples


    . logistic diabetes bmi age sex cholesterol sbp crp smoking drinking
    . confnd, eform
    . confnd, eform(Odds ratio) xline(1)
