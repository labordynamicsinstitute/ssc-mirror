-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
help for circvm
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Fit von Mises distribution to circular data


        circvm varlist [if exp] [in range]


        circvm varname [if exp] [in range] [, by(byvar)]




Description


    circvm fits von Mises (a.k.a. circular normal) distributions to circular
    variables in varlist with scales between 0 and 360 degrees. With a single
    varname and a by() option, distributions are fitted separately for groups
    defined by the distinct values of byvar.


    The parameters fitted are the vector mean and the concentration parameter
    kappa, calculated from the vector strength R by


        2 * R + R^3 + 5 * R^5 / 6,              R <  0.53; 
        -0.4 + 1.39 * R + 0.43 / (1 - R),       R <  0.85; 
        1 / (R^3 - 4 * R^2 + 3 * R),            R >= 0.85. 


    See Fisher (1993) for details and discussion.




Options


    by() indicating grouping is allowed with a single varname.




Examples


    . circvm wallasp axisasp
 
    . circvm wallasp, by(grade)




References


    Fisher, N.I. 1993. Statistical analysis of circular data.  Cambridge:
        Cambridge University Press.
